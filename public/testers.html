Tester Guide

Install RealDate on your phone for the best experience.

Add to Home Screen

  1. Open RealDate in your browser.
  2. Tap the menu and choose Add to Home Screen or Install App.
  3. Confirm the installation.

When you open the installed app allow notifications to get updates and matches.

Offline Test

  1. Open a profile while online so the clips are cached.
  2. Enable airplane mode and reopen the app from your home screen.
  3. Verify that the previously viewed clips still play.
  4. Disable airplane mode and send a chat message to confirm the app reconnects.
